Current Series

Last Words.

In the final moments of His earthly journey, Jesus spoke words that resonate through eternity, embodying love, forgiveness, and redemption. As we conclude our series reflecting on His crucifixion, let us remember His last words: "Father, forgive them, for they do not know what they are doing" (Luke 23:34). In this profound act of mercy, we find the essence of our faith—forgiveness, grace, and the promise of new beginnings. May these words echo in our hearts, inspiring us to extend forgiveness, show compassion, and live lives of love, mirroring the transformative power of Christ's sacrifice
